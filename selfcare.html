Psychiatrist

A psychiatrist is a medical doctor specializing in diagnosing and treating mental health disorders. They can prescribe medication and provide therapy for conditions like depression, anxiety, and schizophrenia. Psychiatrists assess both the mental and physical aspects of psychological issues, offering comprehensive care.
